About CvSU Carmona

Cavite State University (CvSU) is one of the leading state universities in the Philippines, known for its commitment to Truth, Excellence, and Service. Founded in 1906, CvSU has grown into a multi-campus institution offering diverse programs in agriculture, engineering, education, health sciences, business, and more. With a strong focus on academic excellence, research, and community development, CvSU continues to shape globally competitive and socially responsible graduates.

The Carmona Campus, established in 1994, serves as one of the university's extension campuses providing quality education to students in the southern part of Cavite. The campus is strategically located in the fast-growing industrial town of Carmona, offering programs that cater to the needs of local industries and communities.

Mission

Cavite State University shall provide excellent, equitable and relevant educational opportunities in the arts, sciences and technology through quality instruction and responsive research and development activities. It shall produce professional, skilled and morally upright individuals for global competitiveness.

Vision

The premier university in historic Cavite globally recognized for excellence in character development, academics, research, innovation and sustainable community engagement.
